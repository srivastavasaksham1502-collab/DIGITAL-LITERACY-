# Stay Safe Online Checklist

* #### Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
* #### Always check the payment request before approving
* #### Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
* #### Verify the sender before making any payment
* #### Use only trusted and official apps
* #### Enable screen lock and app security
* #### Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
* #### Double-check UPI ID before sending money
* #### Keep your apps updated regularly
* #### Immediately report fraud at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
