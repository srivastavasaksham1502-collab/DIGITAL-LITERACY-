# CASE STUDY



## Topic: UPI / Online Payment Fraud

## 

##### UPI fraud has become very common in recent years, especially among students and people who frequently use digital payment apps. In this type of cybercrime, the attacker tries to trick the victim into sending money or sharing sensitive information like OTP or UPI PIN.

##### 

##### Usually, the fraud starts with a phone call, message, or fake link. The fraudster may pretend to be a bank official, customer care executive, or even a known person. They create a sense of urgency and convince the victim to click on a link or approve a payment request. Sometimes, they send a “collect request” instead of a payment, and if the user approves it, money gets deducted instantly.

##### 

##### Students and new users are easy targets because they may not fully understand how these scams work. The consequences can include financial loss, stress, and loss of trust in digital systems.

##### 

##### This type of fraud shows how important it is to stay alert and verify everything before taking any action online.
